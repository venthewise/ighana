üöÄ How It Works (DEMO)

Complete step-by-step guide to automate your Instagram posting

1 Enter Your Topic
Start by entering a topic or idea in the text area. This will be used to generate relevant captions and images.
2 Generate Content
Click "Generate" to send your topic to our AI system. The N8N backend will process your request and create multiple caption options and images.
3 Select Content
Choose one caption and one image from the generated options. Both selections are required before you can proceed to scheduling.
4 Schedule Posting
Pick a future date and time for your Instagram post. The system will automatically post on schedule.
5 Note for this Version
For direct demo of the workflow, the scheduling logic has not been implemented yet. It will be available on the next demo version.
