Complete guide to using HANA - your AI-powered Instagram automation platform

Overview

HANA (Instagram Automation) is a professional SaaS platform that leverages artificial intelligence to streamline Instagram content creation and scheduling. Our platform combines cutting-edge AI technology with intuitive design to help content creators, businesses, and influencers maintain consistent, engaging social media presence.

The platform integrates with N8N workflow automation and AI services to generate high-quality captions and images based on user-defined topics, then schedules posts for optimal engagement timing.

Advanced AI algorithms generate engaging captions and relevant images tailored to your brand and audience.

Secure & Private

Enterprise-grade security with encrypted data transmission and secure authentication.

Responsive Design

Fully responsive interface that works seamlessly across desktop, tablet, and mobile devices.

Getting Started

Prerequisites

Before using HANA, ensure you have:

  • A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
  • Active internet connection
  • Instagram business or creator account (for posting)

Quick Start Guide

1

Access the Platform

Navigate to the HANA dashboard and log in with your credentials. The default login is sent to your inbox for demo purposes.

2

Enter Your Topic

In the content generation section, enter a topic or theme for your Instagram post. Be specific to get better results.

3

Generate Content

Click the "Generate" button to create AI-powered captions and images based on your topic.

4

Select & Schedule

Choose your preferred caption and image, then select a posting time. The platform supports multiple timezones.

5

Submit & Automate

Submit your selection and let HANA handle the automated posting to your Instagram account.

Features

Content Generation

Our AI-powered content generation creates:

  • Captions: Engaging, relevant text optimized for Instagram engagement
  • Images: High-quality visuals in 9:16 aspect ratio perfect for Instagram Stories and Reels
  • Hashtags: Trending and relevant hashtags included in captions

Smart Scheduling

Advanced scheduling features include:

  • Real-time Clock: Live display of current times in multiple locations
  • Future Scheduling: Schedule posts weeks or months in advance

User Interface

Modern, intuitive interface with:

  • Dark/Light Theme: Toggle between themes for comfortable viewing
  • Custom Captions: Add your own captions alongside AI-generated ones
  • Visual Selection: Easy-to-use interface for selecting content
  • Progress Tracking: Real-time feedback on generation and submission status

Workflow Integration

The complete automation workflow follows this streamlined process:

User Input → N8N Backend → AI Content Generation → User Selection → Scheduling → Instagram Posting

This end-to-end automation ensures consistent, high-quality content delivery with minimal manual intervention.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

Content Generation Fails

If content generation fails, check your internet connection and try again. The AI service may be temporarily unavailable. Check the status of our service providers:

  • OpenAI Status
  • Higgsfield AI Status
  • N8N Status

Login Issues

For demo purposes, use the credentials sent to your inbox to check if you're using the right login information.

Browser Compatibility

HANA works best with modern browsers:

  • Chrome 90+
  • Firefox 88+
  • Safari 14+
  • Edge 90+

Performance Tips

  • Use specific, descriptive topics for better AI results
  • Schedule posts during optimal engagement times
  • Clear browser cache if experiencing issues
